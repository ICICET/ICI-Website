TECHNICAL TALK-WIND ENGINEERING

An informative session to a relatively important and new field was handled Dr. K Suresh Kumar, MD RWDI India. An eminent speaker and an expert in the field of wind engineering, he shared with the student members his works and experiences of studying in foreign universities and working in the RWDI, a Canadian company. The speaker was instrumental in bringing a part of the RWDI, a prominent company in wind engineering, structural and a major consultant to India. He explained the technical facts underlying the process of determining wind coefficients and structural stability of large structures. He also explained the major works carried out under his leadership in RWDI. The session was an enlightening one for many students who were aspiring to be structural engineers. The speaker, Dr. Suresh kumar K is also an alumni of the department of civil engineering and was honoured on the occasion as part of back to department programme of ICI CET for bringing glory to his Alma mater.

INDUSTRIAL VISIT

POABS

The industrial visit to POABS M sand and RMC unit was conducted on 24th September 2016. The team, consisted of 50 from s3 and s5 semester students. The students visited the M Sand manufacturing unit and ready mix concrete manufacturing unit at Muttathara. Information on the details of sieve sizes, crushing methods, various chemicals used etc were made available to them by the instructors present. The Process of production of M sand was seen by the students and has enabled the students to gain knowledge first-hand. The students interacted with various officials present there for a detailed know-how of the working of the unit.

AKKULAM BRIDGE

As part of ICI CET student chapter activities, 41 students visited Aakkulam Bridge during its construction phase. The staff coordinator Prof Biju V, accompanied the students for the visit. The client of the project is National Highway authority of India. The bridge was designed by KNRC. The bridge was being constructed across the Aakkulam River. The students got a clear idea of the type and dimensions of piles used in the construction. The students witnessed the concreting and spot welding techniques. They got an idea on the soil exploration methods, reinforcement details, and also the Metallic girder bearing structures from the site engineers. Prof Biju V also clarified the students through various doubts and clarifications. The students had both an enjoyable and informative session during the visit.

2) REPAIR AND REHABILITATION WORK

25 March 2017
ICI CET STUDENTS’ CHAPTER conducted a one-day camp in the village of Mariyanad as part of the Chapter's Social Service Wing Coordinated by Aminul Hassan and Faseeh. It provided an opportunity for the students in gaining practical skills and to be exposed to the rural areas. There were about 35 students belonging to second, third and fourth years from the Civil Department. The objective of the camp was to undertake some electrical, plumbing and civil works of the Government L P School in Maryanad village. The activities kick started at 9 am in the morning. The students were divided into 3 groups, with each group undertaking a specific work. The first group carried out the plumbing work where students built a new washing area. The second group carried out the electrical works and the exhaust was attached to the kitchen. The third group took the initiative to build the compound wall in the school.Some of the students were engaged in cooking food for the students. Even though with the limited facilities, they managed to prepare tasty and healthy food for breakfast and lunch.
The students faced a lot of challenges but were able to overcome them due to their hard work and dedication for the cause. The students can be proud for their contribution in nurturing the future hopes of the nation's foundation.We are extremely thankful to Prof.Biju Sir for his presence and guidance during the activity. The camp ended by 10.00 pm with the completion of all the undertaken works.
